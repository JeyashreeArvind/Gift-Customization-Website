Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of gifts do you offer?

We offer a wide range of gifts for all occasions—birthdays, anniversaries, weddings, corporate events, and more. Our collection includes personalized items, luxury hampers, home décor, custom keepsakes, and curated gift boxes.

2. Do you offer gift wrapping or personalized messages?

Yes! We offer free gift wrapping and the option to include a personalized message with every order. Simply select the option during checkout.

3. How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary depending on your location and the product. Standard shipping usually takes 3-7 business days, while express shipping is available for 1-3 day delivery. Custom/personalized items may take an extra 1-2 days for processing.
